### Who We Are

Santa Fe Goat Guys (Double M Ranching LLC) is a family-owned, owner-operated targeted-grazing and wildfire mitigation company based in Santa Fe. We operate a 35-head targeted grazing herd with Great Pyrenees livestock guardian dogs, portable electric fencing, and daily on-site herd management, alongside a Wildfire Readiness Division providing assessments, treatment planning, coordinated execution, and insurance-ready documentation. Our technology platform, **FireReady.ai**, generates FEMA-data-anchored wildfire risk assessments and zone-by-zone reports for any New Mexico address.

### Why This Matters to Your SB 33 Application

The Wildfire Prepared Act funds specific activities — and your application needs a contractor who can deliver them. We map directly onto the statute:

| SB 33 Provision | What the Law Funds | SFGG Capability |
|---|---|---|
| §4.A.1.b | *"Hazardous fuels reduction to provide buffers"* | Targeted goat grazing — removes fine fuels on steep, rocky, machine-inaccessible terrain; creates the buffer zones defined in §2.B with zero soil disturbance, at 55–75% below mechanical clearing cost |
| §4.A.1.c | *"Community-based programs to conduct assessments and provide certification"* | FireReady.ai — scalable, professionally administered risk assessments ($20–$30/home at community scale) with FEMA NRI data, photo documentation, and PDF reports for every parcel |
| §2.A, §2.P | *Assessments against wildfire preparedness standards* | On-site professional assessments aligned to IBHS Wildfire Prepared Home — the national standard SB 33 names as the state benchmark (§3.E.4); IBHS assessor certification in progress, summer 2026 |
| §3.E.2.b | *Noncombustible five-foot zone around structures* | Zone 0 conversion scoped as a standard line item in every treatment plan |
| §4.A.2 | *Qualified entities assisting eligible property owners* | Turnkey homeowner delivery: assessment → treatment plan → execution (grazing + vetted licensed partners for tree work) → before/after documentation packaged for certification, grants, and insurers |
